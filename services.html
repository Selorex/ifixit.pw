Before we get started on your device, it's always a good idea to make sure the following are checked.

  • Is your device still under warranty? Certain service providers or manufactures will void any warranty if the device is tampered with outside of their service and contract agreement. Please see where your device stands before bringing it in.

  • Before we repair your device, please make sure to back it up. (If you need help doing this, please contact us.)

  • If there is sensitive information on the phone and the repair requires soft-repairs, please backup your device and remove any sensitive information.
